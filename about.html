About Us

Welcome to Alka Art Studio

A vibrant space where creativity meets craftsmanship. We are passionate about celebrating handmade art and providing a platform for artistic expression and inspiration.

Our Founder

Alka, a seasoned artist with over 25 years of experience, brings a rich blend of tradition, skill, and innovation to our studio. She is a specialist art teacher, known for her expertise across diverse art forms, from intricate handicrafts to captivating paintings. Her passion is not just in creating but also in sharing her skills through workshops.

Our Mission

At Alka Art Studio, we believe that art is for everyone. Whether you're here to learn, explore, or shop for one-of-a-kind handmade treasures, our mission is to foster creativity and connect people through the joy of the handmade.
