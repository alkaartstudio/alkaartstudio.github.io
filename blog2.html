Exploring Indian Art Forms: From Madhubani to Tanjore

From the intricate Madhubani paintings of Bihar to the grandiose Tanjore paintings of Tamil Nadu, Indian art is a rich tapestry of history and storytelling.

Each regional style brings forward deep symbolism, vibrant color palettes, and unique methods, offering a glimpse into India's cultural heritage.
