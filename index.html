About Us

Welcome to Alka's Art Studio – a vibrant space where creativity meets craftsmanship. We are passionate about celebrating handmade art and providing a platform for artistic expression and inspiration.

Founded by Alka, a seasoned artist with over 25 years of experience, our studio brings a rich blend of tradition, skill, and innovation. Alka is a specialist art teacher with Pidilite Industries and a guest lecturer at a government college, known for her expertise across diverse art forms. From intricate handicrafts to captivating paintings, she not only teaches but also shares her creations with the world.

At Alka's Art Studio, we believe art is for everyone — whether you're here to learn, explore, or shop for one-of-a-kind handmade treasures.
