About Us

Welcome to Alka's Art Studio, a space where creativity meets craftsmanship! My name is Alka, and I've been immersed in the world of art for over two decades. With a deep passion for both traditional and contemporary forms of art, I’ve dedicated myself to mastering various mediums, from painting and sculpture to mixed-media creations. At Alka's Art Studio, art isn't just something I create—it's a way of life, a method of self-expression, and a journey of exploration. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of teaching and inspiring countless individuals through workshops and vocational training. As a registered Fevicryl teacher for over 15 years, I have shared my knowledge and love for art with students, encouraging them to think outside the box and experiment with unique materials and techniques. This online gallery serves as an extension of my passion for creativity, offering a collection of my latest works and the opportunity to purchase and enjoy art that can brighten up any space. Each piece in the collection reflects my personal journey, artistic expression, and deep connection to the materials I work with. I believe that art has the power to transform both spaces and souls, which is why every creation is carefully crafted with attention to detail and love. Whether you're looking to add a special piece of artwork to your collection, seeking inspiration, or simply admiring the beauty of handmade creations, I hope my art brings joy, creativity, and positive energy to your life. Feel free to browse through my gallery, and thank you for being a part of this artistic journey with me.
