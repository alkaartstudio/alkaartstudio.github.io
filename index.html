Our Story

Alka Art Studio is a vibrant space where creativity meets craftsmanship.


Our founder: Alka, is a seasoned artist with over 25 years of experience, known for her expertise across diverse art forms, from intricate handicrafts to captivating paintings. Her passion is not just in creating but also in sharing her skills through workshops.


Our Mission: At Alka Art Studio, we believe that art is for everyone. Whether you're here to learn, explore, or shop for one-of-a-kind handmade treasures.
